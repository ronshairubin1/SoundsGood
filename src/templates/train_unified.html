Why use Unified Training?
This training method ensures that the same feature extraction process is used during both training and inference, leading to more accurate predictions.

Key benefits:

  • Eliminates inconsistencies between training and inference features
  • Improves model accuracy by ensuring features are identical
  • Provides better performance for real-time sound classification
  • Simplifies the preprocessing pipeline and reduces errors
